You are organizing your wedding, the documentation team is asking 40k for the wedding package. They will use an Iphone 16 pro max to cover the event and CapCut for the same day edit video. The documentation team has 2 years of experience and has an above average portfolio. Would you agree to that deal? Explain your answer and justify the integrity of their work. Reflect on the integrity, creativity, skill level of the “vibe” coders.

Yes, I would agree if the documentation team demonstrates the integrity and skill level needed to justify the price. Despite using tools that may seem less professional, it does not determine the output of their work. What matters is the skill and the knowledge that the team has in capturing and editing the photos. Same goes with programming. If the programmer isn't knowledgable with programming and completely uses AI to finish the work, they would not have the skill, knowledge, and experience to make it a bug-free and secure program.
